The Last Summer

It's summer on the rugged Scottish island of St Kilda, and free-spirited Effie Gillies is taking gorgeous Lord Sholto about the island. Sholto arrives to give her a position on his estate, and their differences appear insurmountable, even as their attraction grows. Then there's a shocking discovery on St Kilda, and a bright new life is tainted by sinister truths.
