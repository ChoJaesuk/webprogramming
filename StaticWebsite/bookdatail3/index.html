The Maiden

October 1679, Edinburgh. Lady Christian is arrested and accused with the death of James Forrester, her boyfriend. The news of her arrest and following trial is widely publicized, with headlines that leave little room for doubt: Adulteress. Whore. Murderess. Lady Christian had just recently married and was living a life of affluence and respectability. What compelled her to put everything on the line for an affair? And does it make her a murderer? Inspired by a true tale, this is a stunning drama with a feminist revisionist twist that gives a voice to women who have been silenced by history.
