The Maze Runner

James Dashner's The Maze Runner is a young adult dystopian fiction. The first novel in the series follows Thomas, a young boy who wakes up in a mysterious place called the Glade with no knowledge of his history other than his name. The Glade is a huge area surrounded by tall stone walls, with the only way out being through a maze.
